Welcome to Skills Masters Africa

Skills Masters is the global home of excellence for design-led thinking. We are a team of passionate and dedicated individuals from various fields brought together with a shared vision of equipping young people with prerequisite skills to prepare them for a digital future. Our mission is to empower our learners with skills through interactive learning and encourage them to develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills at a very early age. We aim to empower learners from all walks of life with skills that enable them to be global citizens in the digital world. Our curriculum is carefully desined to impart critical thinking and problem solving skills to our learners creating solutions in a dynamic environment. Our mission is to promote this mindset shift by making design-led thinking accessible to everyone, and a standard practice.

Skills Masters Code Festivals

This is the premiere kids coding competition in Kenya. CODEFEST is the biggest coding Fest hosted annually by SKILLSMASTERS KE, where participants make innovative projects with hands-on learning using PictoBlox and other basic app development platforms to create solutions to real life situations. Through training sessions and workshops, students can explore the exciting world of AI, Machine Learning, Coding, and Robotics. CODEFST 1.0 motivates participants to engage in interactive activities through learn-by-doing and doubt-solving sessions. Thereby promoting the development of critical thinking, creativity, collaboration, and communication, the four Cs of the 21st-Century Skills! CODEFEST 1.0, is aligned with the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals. This creates a purpose driven environment that allows young developers to channel their AI and coding skills towards solving real-life problems and unlock their creative and innovative skills for a sustainable future

Partnerships with Schools

We are partnering with various schools to initiate coding clubs and equip the learners with necessary skills that will make them ready for a technology filled future. Currently we are in four counties covering a total of ten schools .

Coding Bootcamps

These are intensive short term programs. The trainings are planned periodically during school holidays and learners are taken through thorough training for a period of between two weeks to five weeks. 2023 we had a bootcamp at PAC University, Roysambu Campus. 2024, We are a planning for Code with Lions Bootcamp in Maasai Mara.
Learners enjoy the learning process since we incorporate so many fun activities that allows learners to code throughout the day without fatigue.
We have Zumba dance sessions which sometimes involve parents and guardians.
2024, it will be BIG! Code with Lions.....BOOK YOUR SLOT NOW

Year Long Programs

This program is tailored to offer coding classes throughout the year. The lessons are conducted virtually allowing learners the flexible opportunity to learn from home and still enjoy top notch skill based training. The program gives you full support and unlocks tremendous potential in the learner. Currently we have over 500 learners from five countries with Kenya having the bulk of learners. Other countries we have presence include Uganda, Rwanda, Ethiopia and United Kingdom.

Coding Exhibitions & Competitions

We organize annual coding exhibitions & competitions among kids in partnership with leading industry organisations This offers learners an opportunity to develop their projects, document them and thereafter make a presentation of the same. We believe this is key to developing the entrepreneurial skills of learners at an early age and build confidence going into the future.
